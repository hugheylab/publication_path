Hello, and thank you for checking out C(3P)O! The purpose of this application is to allow authors to enter the submission path that their papers went through prior to publication. This information will be used to create a database of submission paths, available for anyone to use and analyze. The article information we have comes from pubmed (via pmdb) and pubmed central. First, search for articles by email address(es) attached to the paper or DOI / pubmed IDs (PMID). Second, select email address(es) for each article you wish to enter information for, and we will generate a unique URL to enter the submission path and send it to the selected address(es). Finally, use the link in the email to enter the submission path and (optionally) the date you first submitted to each journal. Thank you again for your participation!

*Please note that C(3P)O is currently in beta and is a work in progress.*

Instructions
  • Select whether to search by email address or DOI / PMID.
  • Enter one or more search terms as a comma-separated list.
  • Click Search to search for matching articles and their associated emails.
